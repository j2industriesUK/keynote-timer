Privacy Policy

Keynote Timer — Last updated: April 2025

Overview

Keynote Timer is a simple, self-contained presentation timer. It does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data — about you or anyone else.

Data we collect

None. Keynote Timer collects no data whatsoever.

  • No account registration required.
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • No advertising frameworks.
  • No network requests of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Your timer configurations and saved timers are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Your preferences (haptics, sound, theme) are stored locally in the same way.

Notifications

Keynote Timer may request permission to send local notifications so that you receive alerts (final segment, final minute, time's up) even when the app is backgrounded. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server.

Third-party services

Keynote Timer uses no third-party SDKs, services, or APIs.

Children's privacy

Keynote Timer is rated 4+ on the App Store. Because we collect no data, there are no special considerations regarding children's privacy.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Given the nature of the app, we do not anticipate changes.
